Poem: Prickly Pear

Amidst the sands where dry winds blow,
The prickly pear begins to grow.
With spines so sharp and flesh so sweet,
A hidden treasure in the heat.

Its bright pink flower among the desertscape
is most welcome, always. 

Prickly pear, thank you for making my day.
